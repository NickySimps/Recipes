Good Cheap Banana Bread

Can I make Banana Bread?

This is based on a bread recipe I've grown fond of

Ingredients

  • Dry

    • 2 cups flour
    • 1 cup of sugar
    • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
    • 1 teaspoon of salt

  • Wet

    • 3 large bananas, pureed
    • 4 oz butter
    • 2 eggs

  • Spices

    • Nutmeg
    • Cloves
    • Allspice
    • Cinnamon

How can I Banana Bread?

Imma need you to make that Banana Bread homie

  1. Throw the Dry in the mixing bowl in the order listed
  2. Mix all the wets in another bowl
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  4. Combine ingredients
  5. Bake ~45 minutes
